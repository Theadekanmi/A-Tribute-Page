About The Legend

A.S.O Folajuwon, in full, Akande Samad Olalekan, was born on July 10th, 1992, in Saki, Oyo Stat, Nigeria.

☛ He served as the President of Agricultural Engineering from 2019 to 2021.

☛ Folajuwon earned a Higher National Diploma in Agricultural engineering from The Oke Ogun Polytechnic, Saki(TOPS).

☛ In 2022, he moved to Ibadan City, where he started his Construction Engineering project.

☛ He also played a pivotal organizational, technical, and political role in Saki and Oyo State at large.

☛ His prominent role in helping people, supporting and standing with his friends and family makes him a national hero and role model to many.

☛ He's blessed with a mansion, lovely car, a good business.
